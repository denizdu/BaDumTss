Long-term concept
-----------------

Build a two-stage system:

1. Analyze music and extract tempo, key, rhythm, harmonic, spectral, timbral, and emotional features.
2. Generate or recommend a new backing track from the validated analysis data.

Potential approaches include nearest-neighbor retrieval for recommendations and symbolic sequence models for later MIDI generation. Complex neural models should not be introduced until the training data, labels, evaluation split, and baseline are reliable.

Expected output may include a MIDI arrangement for DAW editing and, later, rendered audio. Users should be able to adjust high-level controls such as energy, darkness, density, and rhythmic complexity.
